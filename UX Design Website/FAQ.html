FAQ



What is the Service Learning Club?

In the Service Learning Club, students are able to give back to the community by providing services, such as cleaning public spaces, taking care of the elderly and people in need, and earn CCA points.












How do I know if this club is for me?

If you enjoy helping others in need, spending time with your peers to do meaningful activities, and brightening other people's days, this club is for you!












What is the time commitment?

The time commitment is flexible. We offer a mix of one-time events and ongoing projects. You can participate in as many or as few activities as your schedule allows. We do encourage regular participation to get the most out of the experience.
